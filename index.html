Short Biography

I’m a dependable and detail-oriented student who enjoys solving problems and learning through building. In group work, I focus on clear communication, sharing progress early, and being respectful of deadlines and teammates’ time. I like environments where expectations are clear and feedback is used to improve.

I’m comfortable presenting my work, explaining decisions, and updating documents so others can follow along. I bring a calm, consistent approach to tasks, even when timelines are tight. Outside of class projects, I’m motivated by work that has a real user in mind. I’m looking for opportunities where I can support a team, keep learning, and take ownership of small-to-medium features end to end.
